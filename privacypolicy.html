Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website. Personally Identifiable Information (PII), as defined by U.S. privacy law and information security, refers to any information that can be used on its own or with other details to identify, contact, or locate an individual. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When you register, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, respond to a survey, or fill out forms on our website, we may collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Credit card or payment details
  • Other optional information to improve your experience

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used in the following ways:

  • To personalize your user experience and deliver relevant content
  • To improve our website functionality and design
  • To respond more effectively to customer service requests
  • To process transactions quickly and securely
  • To send periodic emails about services, improvements, or new products

Protection of Information

Our website is regularly scanned for security vulnerabilities, and we use malware scanning to ensure safety. Sensitive data, such as credit card information, is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and stored securely behind restricted-access networks.

Multiple security measures are in place whenever you place an order, submit, or access information to safeguard your personal data.

Cookies

Yes, we use cookies. Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow our system to recognize your browser, remember certain preferences, and improve your shopping experience. They help us:

  • Remember and process items in your shopping cart
  • Understand site traffic and interactions to enhance future experiences

You may choose to accept or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some features, but you will still be able to place orders.

Third Parties

We do not sell or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent. However, we may release information if required by law, to enforce our site policies, or to protect our rights or others’ safety.

Non-personal information may be shared with outside parties for marketing, advertising, or analytics purposes.

Occasionally, we may feature third-party products or services on our website. These third parties have their own independent privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

Google’s advertising requirements are guided by their Advertising Principles. We do not currently use Google AdSense, but may do so in the future.

California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA)

CalOPPA requires commercial websites to post a privacy policy that discloses the information being collected and how it is shared. In compliance with CalOPPA:

  • Users can visit our site anonymously.
  • We will provide a link to this Privacy Policy on our homepage or the first significant page after entering our site.
  • The link includes the word "Privacy" and is easy to locate.

Users will be notified of changes to this Privacy Policy:

  • Via email

Users can update their personal information:

  • By logging in to their account

Do Not Track Signals

We honor “Do Not Track” signals from your browser. When this setting is enabled, we do not track, plant cookies, or use advertising mechanisms.
